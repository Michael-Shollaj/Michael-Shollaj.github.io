Valhalla Awaits Me
2D platformer Viking game for mobile, quite challenging, kill enemies, collect orb to open the door and go to another level, with cool graphics
Stars : 5

Valhalla Awaits Me was built from scratch, and it took me 8 months to create this game

Game contains enemies with different behavior and different obstacles, which makes the game more difficult and challenging

Player should collect all orbs to open the door to go to another level, but to do this, long journey waits for player

This game is about a viking, who wants to go to his family, but enemies would try everything to not allow him to go to his family, so we have to defeat them, and we meet family

Knight Avenger
Very fun 2d platformer game. You have to kill a lot of enemies, collect coins, buy new stronger character to kill more easily enemies, avid the obstacles, buy health and arrows to kill enemies with bow :D
Stars : Not yet

This games is about a warrior, who is left alone, and his goal is to kill all his enemies that wants to kill him

Game contains 38 levels, 5 final bosses, many types of enemies for example, flying enemies like evil birds and bats, grounded enemies like Skeletons, Zombies, Demons etc

Goal of the player is to kill a lot of enemies, grab key and open doors, collect coins so he upgrades himself and be stronger

This game was made with Unity, and for art was used Inkscape, animation was made with Unity
