2DPlayerController


Free 2D controller plugin, where your player can walk, jump, climbing ladder, wall jump, make key follows you and opens door with cool animation, and great effects from Jmo Assets. This package includes also enemies script, platform movements and falling platforms. I hope you enjoy it :))
