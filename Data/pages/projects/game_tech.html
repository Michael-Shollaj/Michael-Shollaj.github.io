Advanced Character Controller2D


Advanced controller, the player has the ability to walk, double jump, combat system, crouch, wall jump, and so on, the player can also grab keys and open doors, the package contains also moving platforms, falling platforms

Also, the combat system is very advanced, the player can do 3-4 or more attack combinations, and we can also add a slow-motion effect so that the combat system can be felt more

You can download it at the Itch Io logo above, press the download button, unzip the folder, and add it to your project
