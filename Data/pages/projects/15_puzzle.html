IZ'UX
IZ'UX an alien that tries to escape from Earth, but is impossible, because bots and seagullz notice him and tries to kill him
Stars : Not yet

Alien at the game, flies when you touch the screen, and when you don't touch it, he falls down, try to not make alien falls to low, and avoid all robots and seagullz, also collect powerups

Goal of the player is to stay alive as long as possible, to not touch enemies

This game was made with Unity, and for art was used Inkscape, animation was made with Unity
